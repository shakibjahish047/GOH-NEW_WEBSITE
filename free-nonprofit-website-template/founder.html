Our Founder

LAL MOHAMMAD DARWISH

Lal Mohammad Darwish, born in 1993 in Balkh province, char bolak district is the founder of Gate of Hope Organization. He completed his education in Mazar-e-Sharif and holds a Bachelor’s degree in Social Sciences, along with professional training in English, computer programs, and leadership. Mr. Darwish has more than 20 years of experience in social work, community development, and youth empowerment. He has worked with several national and international organizations, including WHO, CARE, ACTED, and the Afghanistan Independent Human Rights Commission, where he led programs focused on legal awareness, good governance, and community engagement. As founder, he has played a key role in shaping Gate of Hope Organization’s mission to support youth, strengthen families, and build resilient communities. His vision is centered on providing opportunities, skills, and hope for young people across Afghanistan.

Every Afghan deserves hope and opportunity. This is why we started Gate of Hope — to empower communities through education, support, and dignity.

As the CEO/Founder of Gate of Hope, I take immense pride in our assembly of an esteemed cadre of professionals. Each member of our team embodies dedication, commitment, and unparalleled expertise, meticulously chosen to advance our mission. Our foremost aim is to not only meet but surpass the expectations vested in us, through the provision of exemplary services. In our relentless pursuit of excellence, we are continuously at the forefront of adopting innovative methodologies and cutting-edge technologies. This ensures our operations are executed with utmost efficiency and effectiveness. Our ethos of perpetual growth and enhancement compels us to seek novel strategies, thereby elevating the caliber of our offerings.
At the heart of Gate of Hope lies a profound belief: our success is intricately linked to the contentment of our doners. It is through the fortitude of our partnerships that we gauge our progress. Driven by an indomitable passion to contribute to national advancement, our commitment to excellence is resolute

Founder Activites

In 2019, the bustling streets of Balkh province witnessed an event that marked a significant milestone in the humanitarian landscape of Afghanistan. It was the inauguration of the main office of Gate of Hope. The event was not just a mere opening of an office space; it symbolized hope, commitment, and a promise to uplift and support those in dire need.

In a significant display of unwavering commitment and collaboration, the leadership of Gate of Hope NGO recently held a crucial meeting with the Afghan team. The discussion centered around extending and enhancing services for our fellow countrymen in Afghanistan, particularly in the relevant provinces where Gate of Hope operates. This meeting not only reaffirms Gate of Hope’s dedication to making a difference but also promises new horizons of collaboration.

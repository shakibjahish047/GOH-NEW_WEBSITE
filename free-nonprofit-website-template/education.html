Our Work in Education

Gate of Hope is deeply committed to providing education opportunities for children and youth across Afghanistan. We believe education is the foundation for a brighter future and sustainable peace. Our programs support school supplies, teacher training, and literacy courses to ensure every child has access to quality learning.

By investing in education, we aim to empower the next generation with skills and knowledge that enable them to contribute positively to their communities and country.
